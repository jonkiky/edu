Equestrian

Equestrian sports involve riding horses in various disciplines, such as dressage, show jumping, and eventing. Riders demonstrate control, skill, and harmony with their horses.

Skiing

Skiing includes various disciplines such as alpine skiing, Nordic skiing, and freestyle skiing. Athletes navigate downhill courses or perform tricks on snow.

Beach Volleyball

Beach volleyball is a variation of indoor volleyball played on sandy courts. Teams of two players aim to score by sending the ball over the net and into the opponent's court.

Water Polo

Water polo is a team sport played in a swimming pool. Teams aim to score by throwing a ball into the opposing team's goal. It involves swimming, passing, and shooting.

Bowling

Bowling is a recreational and competitive sport where players roll a heavy ball down a lane to knock down pins. It requires precision and consistency.

Rifle

Rifle shooting is a precision sport where participants use rifles to hit targets from a fixed distance. It demands focus, accuracy, and control.

Fencing

Fencing is a combat sport where participants use swords (foils, epees, or sabres) for competitive dueling. It requires precision, strategy, and agility.

Gymnastics

Gymnastics involves a combination of physical strength, flexibility, balance, and coordination. Athletes perform routines on apparatus such as the floor, bars, and beam.

Rowing

Rowing is a water sport where teams or individuals propel boats using oars. It requires synchronized rowing and strength, with races typically held on rivers or lakes.

Ice Hockey

Ice hockey is a fast-paced team sport played on ice. Teams aim to score by shooting the puck into the opposing team's net. It combines physicality and skill on skates.

Field Hockey

Field hockey is a team sport played with a ball and curved sticks. Teams aim to score by hitting the ball into the opposing team's goal. It involves skillful dribbling and passing.

Lacrosse

Lacrosse is a team sport played with a small rubber ball and a long-handled stick. Teams aim to score by shooting the ball into the opposing team's goal. It combines physicality and finesse.

Tennis

Tennis is a racket sport played on a rectangular court divided by a net. Players aim to score points by hitting the ball over the net and into the opponent's court. It involves agility and precision.

Golf

Golf is an individual sport played on a golf course. Players aim to complete the course in as few strokes as possible by hitting a ball into a series of holes. It requires both skill and strategy.

Swimming and Diving

Swimming involves racing in water using various strokes, while diving focuses on performing acrobatic maneuvers from diving boards. Both sports require skill and precision in the water.

Track and Field

Track and field includes various athletic events such as running, jumping, and throwing. Athletes compete in races, long jumps, high jumps, shot put, and more.

Cross Country

Cross country is a long-distance running sport where athletes race over natural terrain. It tests endurance and stamina as runners traverse challenging courses.

Wrestling

Wrestling is a combat sport where competitors aim to control and pin their opponents. It involves grappling techniques and strength.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport played on a rectangular court with a net. Teams aim to score by sending the ball over the net and into the opponent's court. It requires precise passing, setting, and spiking.

Soccer

Soccer, also known as football in many parts of the world, is a team sport played with a spherical ball. Teams aim to score by kicking the ball into the opposing team's goal. It's known for its endurance and skill.

Softball

Softball is similar to baseball but played with a larger ball on a smaller field. It involves pitching, hitting, and fielding, emphasizing both skill and teamwork.

Baseball

Baseball is a bat-and-ball sport where players aim to score by hitting a pitched ball and running around bases. It's known for its precision and strategy.

Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced sport played with a round ball. Teams aim to score by shooting the ball through the opposing team's hoop. It involves dribbling, passing, and teamwork.

Football

Football is a team sport played with an oval-shaped ball. Teams aim to score by carrying the ball into the opposing team's end zone or kicking it through the goalposts. It's known for its physicality and strategic gameplay.

Racing car

Racing car activities feature high-speed competitions with various vehicles, from Formula 1 to off-road racing, providing thrilling experiences for enthusiasts and drivers.
